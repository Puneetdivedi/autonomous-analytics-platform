Executive answer

Analysis shows a clear difference across segments, with North America accounting for the majority of total revenue and its month-over-month decline.

  • North America contributes ~71% of total revenue and drove the period’s decline.
  • The drop is a volume effect — units fell while unit price held steady.
  • EU revenue was resilient, moving only marginally versus the prior month.

Root cause: demand softness in North America rather than discounting or pricing — consistent with the computed statistics.
